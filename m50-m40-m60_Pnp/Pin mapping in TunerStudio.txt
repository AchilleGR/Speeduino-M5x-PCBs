As Board Layout choose "Speeduino v0.4" and most of the pins will be "board default"

But in addition to that:

- Baro sensor: A5
- Vanos/VVT in m50tu: D11
- DISA in m4x: D30
- CEL light on instrument cluster: D26
- Spare relay output on external connector: D28
- Spare relay output on second external connector: D30 & D32
- A/C idle up input pin is D21
